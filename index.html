TelaCo is shorthand for Tela Community. It is a haven community for the textile industry stakeholders. We are an Information and Communications Technology (ICT) and data-driven movement that exist to address the ailing and recurring problems of the agriculture industry particularly that of the Natural Textile Industry.

We bring a Business-to-Business Platform to integrate the members of the Natural Textile Supply Chain where the members can check for the supply and demand forecast so they can adjust, calibrate or make business decisions in order to address this Billion Dollar industry.
